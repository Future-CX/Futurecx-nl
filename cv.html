Mediq wants to replace their online order platform in the Nordics to successfully fulfil customer requirements and win new sales opportunities and tenders impacting revenue growth.

Martijn convinced Mediq on the solution by providing product demos and mapping the business requirements to the SAP Commerce Cloud solution.

Martijn designed a Ecommerce solution architecture with integrations to backend systems. After that he led the Rightshore DevOps team technically and functionally to build the solution in 3 phases.

Since the solution was build for mulitple countries and multiple business units in the healthcare sector plus the integrations via middleware were build to legacy backend systems, the technical complexity of this project was very high. Using his knowledge of SAP Commerce Cloud and his expertise in Agile DevOps Martijn was able to deliver the solution to the clients satisfaction.

Mediq really valued the agile approach and alignment of the backlog and solution over multiple business units of Mediq. With is knowledge and experience of SAP Commerce Cloud he could steer the client in requirement workshops.

Medtronic wants to sell their product online to 5500 hospitals in the EMEA region without human interference (touchless) by building a B2B Ecommerce portal.

Martijn was responsible as Solution Architect for gather all business requirements from stakeholders and designing the solution. He aligned all stakeholders on a MVP approach to start small, learn and expand when the budget of the project was cut in half.

As the Ecommerce portal is intended for hospital equipment such as pacemakers, the topics of quality, security and privacy data add complexity to the project. Martijn managed and controlled this extra complexity with his expertise on DevOps.

Medtronic is very happy with the contribution of Martijn in this project and praises his knowledge, collaboration and enthusiasm. He is one of the key factors of the success of this project.

"Martijn has a lot of eCommerce knowledge, he is very pragmatic and is happy to jump in and help where there is a need. He has been very good in picking up new requirements and documenting them and working with all the stakeholders to get them aligned and sign off" - Claudia Leenders, IT Manager Ecommerce, Medtronic

The client spoke with great appreciation of the energy Martijn brought to the team, his technical knowledge and the proposed solutions to the project.
