    How to Read This Ecommerce Trends Radar

    The radar groups ecommerce trends into six domains: Data & AI, Advertising & Promotion, Content & Experience, CRM & Analytics, Commerce & Sales, and Software Architecture. Each trend is positioned by adoption maturity, from late majority at the center to innovators on the outer ring, with laggards (legacy) shown outside the radar to highlight declining approaches.

    Use this page to identify which ecommerce capabilities are already becoming standard, which ones require near-term investment decisions, and which emerging trends deserve monitoring. The strongest themes in 2026 are AI-assisted commerce, composable and event-driven architecture, first-party data activation, self-service journeys, marketplace growth, and payment orchestration.
