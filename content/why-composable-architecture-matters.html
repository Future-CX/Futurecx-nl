Why Composable Architecture Matters

Enterprise Architecture
April 26, 2025
Updated: December 1, 2025
Author
Martijn van Deel
This image was generated using ChatGPT, a generative artificial intelligence chatbot developed by OpenAI.

Composable architecture matters because it transforms the way digital platforms grow and evolve. By breaking the landscape into modular, independent capabilities, organisations gain the freedom to innovate without waiting for the entire system to change. New features can be added faster, outdated components replaced without friction, and customer experiences tailored with far greater agility. It is the foundation that enables continuous improvement, experimentation, and long-term scalability in modern e-commerce.
