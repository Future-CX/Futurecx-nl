My Experience and Services

I started my career as a web developer and grew into an experienced Solution Architect. In the last 15 years, I have worked with Ecommerce solutions, Content Platforms, Digital Marketing applications, and Customer Service solutions. As a Solution Architect, I have designed the solution, created solution diagrams, integration specifications, and data models. When preparing for implementation I created a technology roadmap with a stepped approach: MVP, Improve, Excel.

I worked with many different software partners like Adobe, SAP, Salesforce, Sitecore, CommerceTools, Contentful, and more. The knowledge and experience I have gained, help me to understand the strengths and weaknesses of every solution. This is extremely valuable in software selection processes to find the best solution for the use-cases we need.

I am a true believer in Composable Architecture. This new architecture principle helps organizations to increase go-to-market and early adopt new client expectations with new services, like new channels to interact. Using the Strangler Pattern it is possible to transition from a monolithic platform to a Composable platform while keeping the shop running.
